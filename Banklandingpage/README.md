# Banklandingpage

This folder contains the Yarrow-Mullein Bank landing page. The design emphasizes Indigenous values and the Medicine Wheel, with a soft neumorphism interface and clean, easy-to-follow content.

## Page details
- Yarrow-Mullein Bank is an Indigenous-led mid-size bank formed by the merger of Yarrow Bank and Mullein Bank.
- The landing page highlights Personal Banking, Business Banking, and Wealth Management.
- It also explains the bank’s integration of the Seven Sacred Indigenous Teachings, community commitment, and trust-building principles.
- The page uses a modern neumorphism-inspired design for clarity, balance, and warmth.

## Sections included
- Hero overview
- Services offered
- Indigenous values
- Customer commitment pillars
- CEO leadership quote
- Contact and brand summary
